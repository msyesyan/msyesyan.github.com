I must be alone  
To love  
We must be together  
I think I love you  
When I’m alone  
More than I’m think of you  
When we’re together  

I cannot think   
Without loving  
Or love  
Without think  

Alone I love  
To think of us together  
Together I think  
I’d love to be alone
